1. **Frontend approach** — Are they building their own frontend and calling your API, or do they want to embed your player in their app, or do they want to funnel users to your frontend with their branding?
2. **Integration work** — Who builds the integration? You or their dev team? And does that work happen inside or outside your employed hours?
3. **End-user authentication** — Do they manage their own users and pass you an identifier, or do you provide user accounts on your end?
4. **How many end users** — How many people will be using this in the POC? Tens? Hundreds? This affects infrastructure costs and pricing tier.
5. **Content creation workflow** — Are their people logging into your platform to build programmes directly, or do they want an API for content creation too?
6. **API costs** — Who covers variable costs (TTS generation, storage, compute)? Are they included in the licence fee, or billed separately on usage?
7. **Support expectations** — What do they expect if something breaks? Business hours response? SLA? Or best efforts?
8. **Branding requirements** — How deep does the white-labelling need to go? Just their logo and colours on the player, or full custom domain, custom emails, the lot?
9. **Data and reporting** — Do they need usage analytics back? (Who completed what, how often, compliance reporting for SF/SAR?)
10. **Exclusivity** — Do they want to be the only client using this in the defence/first-responder space

So, it depends on a couple of things
