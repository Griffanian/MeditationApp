## 2. Definitions
- **Platform** — The meditation programme builder, content management system, audio generation pipeline, player, and all associated software and infrastructure owned and maintained by the Provider.
- **Content** — Any meditation programmes, scripts, audio recordings, text, images, or other materials created, uploaded, or assembled by the Creator using the Platform.
- **Raw Assets** — The Creator's original materials: written scripts, uploaded audio files, programme structures, and text. Excludes assembled outputs, platform-generated audio, and platform-specific formatting.
- **Generated Assets** — Audio files produced by the Platform's text-to-speech and audio assembly services based on the Creator's scripts and configuration.
- **Audience** — Individuals who access the Creator's Content through the Platform.
## 3. Platform Access
3.1. The Provider grants the Creator access to the Platform to create, edit, manage, and distribute Content to the Creator's Audience.
3.2. Access is subject to:
  - (a) The Creator maintaining an active account in good standing
  - (b) Payment of applicable fees (see Section 7)
  - (c) Compliance with the acceptable use terms in Section 6
3.3. The Provider may update, modify, or improve the Platform at any time. The Provider will make reasonable efforts to avoid breaking changes that affect existing Content without notice.
## 4. Content Ownership
4.1. **The Creator retains ownership of all Content created by the Creator on the Platform.** This includes scripts, programme structures, uploaded audio, and the creative and intellectual work embodied in the Content.
4.2. **The Platform is and remains the sole intellectual property of the Provider.** The Creator acquires no rights to the Platform's source code, architecture, design, audio processing pipeline, or any other component of the Platform.
4.3. Generated Assets (text-to-speech audio, assembled outputs) are derived works created by the Platform based on the Creator's scripts and configuration. The Creator owns the creative direction and script content. The Provider owns the tools and processes that generated them. For practical purposes:
  - (a) The Creator may use Generated Assets freely as part of their Content on the Platform
  - (b) The Creator may not extract Generated Assets for use on competing platforms without the Provider's written consent
  - (c) If this Agreement terminates, the Creator's rights to Generated Assets are governed by Section 9

5.1. **Creator to Provider:** The Creator grants the Provider a limited, non-exclusive licence to host, store, process, assemble, and serve the Creator's Content solely for the purpose of operating the Platform and delivering Content to the Creator's Audience. This licence exists only for the duration of this Agreement.
5.2. **Provider to Creator:** The Provider grants the Creator a non-exclusive, non-transferable licence to use the Platform's tools, player, and delivery infrastructure for creating and distributing Content, subject to the terms of this Agreement.
5.3. Neither licence grants ownership. The Creator does not own the Platform. The Provider does not own the Content.

6.3. The Provider reserves the right to suspend or remove Content that violates these terms, with reasonable notice where possible.

## 9. Term and Termination
9.1. This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues until terminated by either party.
9.2. Either party may terminate this Agreement at any time by giving [30] days' written notice.
9.3. The Provider may terminate immediately if the Creator materially breaches the acceptable use terms in Section 6.
