# Get started with Granola
### Summary
### Granola Interface Overview

- Notepad on the right: works like a regular text editor, type freely
- Transcript visible via the green dancing bars at the bottom of the screen
  - Click to open and see live voice capture

### Generating Enhanced Notes

- Click the stop button to end the meeting
- Click the bright green “Enhanced Notes” button that appears
- Notes combine transcript content with anything typed in the notepad

### What Granola Delivers

- Beautiful, shareable notes with minimal effort across all meetings
- Auto-detects meeting end and triggers note generation automatically

### Next Steps

- Use Granola across upcoming meetings to build familiarity with the workflow
