# Perfect stillness practice — body awareness and sitting still
### Summary
### 	Practice Overview

- “Perfect Stillness” body awareness practice
- Duration: \~5 minutes
- Seated, spine straight; lying down possible but not recommended

### Setup and Posture

- Sit upright, head and spine straight
- Relax shoulders, let arms hang naturally
- Close eyes to heighten physical sensation awareness
- One final adjustment allowed before formally entering stillness

### The Stillness Practice

- Goal: complete stillness, no movement at all
- Notice body signals: weight, discomfort, urges to shift or scratch
- Itches, hair in face, minor inconveniences: resist all of them
- Breath movement is the one exception
- Personal note: room was too hot during the session
